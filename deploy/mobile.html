PhantomOS Touch Controls

M button = Enable/disable mouse (starts off)
Tap = Move cursor + left click (when mouse on)
T button = Trackpad mode (drag to move cursor)
R button = Right-click (next tap)
Kb button = Special keys bar
Ctrl/Alt = Hold (tap to toggle, red = held)
Two-finger tap = Right click

Tip: Landscape mode gives a better view
